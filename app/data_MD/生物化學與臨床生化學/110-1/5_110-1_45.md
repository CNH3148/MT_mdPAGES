## 題目

A.遺傳性低血磷症(Inherited hypophosphatemia)
B.肝膽道疾病(Hepatobiliary disorders)
C.柏德氏症(Paget disease)
D.急性心肌梗塞(Acute myocardial infarction, AMI)

> [!success]- 正確解答
> **C**
