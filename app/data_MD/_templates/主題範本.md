## 包含題庫

```dataview
TABLE WITHOUT ID
  file.link AS "題目",
  year AS "年份",
  question_number AS "題號",
  difficulty AS "難度",
  key_concept AS "考點",
  is_fixed AS "已掌握",
  summarize_including AS "重點收錄",
  current_answer AS "作答"
FROM ""
WHERE type = "question" AND contains(string(topic), this.file.name)
SORT year DESC, question_number ASC
```
