A.高血壓性腦血管病變(hypertensive cerebrovascular disease)
B.血栓性腦血管栓塞(embolic cerebral infarction)
C.動靜脈畸形(arteriovenous malformation)
D.大腦澱粉樣血管病變(cerebral amyloid angiopathy)

> [!success]- 正確解答
